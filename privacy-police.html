Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Use

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information to ensure the website functions properly and improves your user experience. This includes:

-IP address
-Browser type
-Access times
-Referrer URL

If you register on our website, we collect your name, email address, and password. This data is used to provide you with access to our Kanban platform and its services. The legal basis for this processing is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR for website functionality and Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR for user registration.

Data Protection

Access Controls: Only individuals involved in the educational project have access to your personal data.

Data Minimization: We collect and process only the data necessary for providing our educational services.

Data Retention: Your personal data is retained only for the duration of the educational project or as required by educational regulations.

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless it is necessary to provide our services, legally required, or you have given explicit consent. If you have any questions or concerns about our data protection practices, please contact us a m.guerdal@web.de.
